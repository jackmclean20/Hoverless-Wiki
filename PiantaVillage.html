The beginning of this level without FLUDD is the same as in categories with hover, though it is much more difficult without the hover nozzle to reach the pianta atop the mushroom to collect the shine. There are three different strats you can do here, with varying benefits and difficulties.


TLDR: GWK strat > backflip + triple-jump strat in every way possible


GWK strat:

Start by doing a loose spin into a spin-jump from the bottom left corner of the platform in which FLUDD is obtained toward the middle section of the mushroom facing that direction. When you hit the side of the mushroom cap, a wall slide will occur shortly, where a GWK can be done to allow Mario to grab the ledge on the side of the mushroom.

Backflip strat:

I am unsure when this method was originally discovered, but I at least came up with it in 2019, and it offers a different method to reach the top of the mushroom.
By ledge-grabbing the edge of the post surrounding the base of the mushroom on the left side, shifting to the far left side of it, and performing a backflip while holding forward the entire time, you can dive at the peak of the flip to barely make it atop the mushroom. Make sure when performing the backflip that the spray is aimed fully downward first, as this increases Marioâ€™s vertical speed slightly, increasing the peak height of the backflip by a crucial ~9 units. The trick is not possible if FLUDD is not pointed fully downward.
